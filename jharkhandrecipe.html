Dhuska

Ingredients: Rice, chana dal, ginger, garlic, cumin, green chili, salt, oil, potato curry or ghugni.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Soak rice and chana dal for several hours. Grind with ginger, garlic, cumin, green chili, and salt into a thick batter. Deep-fry spoonfuls until puffed and golden. Serve with potato curry or ghugni.

Rugra Curry

Ingredients: Rugra mushrooms, onion, ginger, garlic, green chili, tomato, mustard oil, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, salt.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Clean rugra mushrooms well. Saute onion, ginger, garlic, green chili, and tomato in mustard oil. Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Add rugra and cook until tender. Serve with rice.

Chilka Roti

Ingredients: Rice, chana dal, salt, oil, chutney or curry.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Soak rice and chana dal, then grind into a smooth batter with salt. Spread thin on a hot tawa and cook like dosa with a little oil. Serve with chutney or curry.

Thekua

Ingredients: Wheat flour, jaggery syrup, fennel, coconut, cardamom, ghee, oil.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Mix wheat flour with jaggery syrup, fennel, grated coconut, cardamom, and ghee. Knead into a stiff dough. Shape into discs with patterns and deep-fry on low heat until brown and crisp. Cool before storing.

Pitha

Ingredients: Rice flour, hot water, chana dal, coconut, jaggery or spiced lentils.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Make rice flour dough with hot water. Prepare stuffing with chana dal, coconut, jaggery, or spiced lentils. Fill, shape, and steam until firm. Serve warm during festivals.

Handia Rice

Ingredients: Rice, ranu fermentation tablets, water.
Time: 3 to 5 days.
Method: Cook rice and cool it. Mix with traditional ranu fermentation tablets and store in a clay pot for several days. Strain and serve as a traditional fermented rice drink during community gatherings.
