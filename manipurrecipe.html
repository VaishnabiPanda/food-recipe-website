Eromba

Ingredients: Potatoes, vegetables, bamboo shoots, fermented fish, roasted chilies, salt, coriander, spring onion, herbs.
Time: 6 to 8 hours plus 30 minutes.
Method: Boil potatoes, vegetables, and bamboo shoots until soft. Mash with fermented fish, roasted chilies, and salt. Mix in chopped coriander, spring onion, and herbs. Serve with rice.

Chamthong

Ingredients: Seasonal vegetables, onion, ginger, garlic, green chili, salt, fermented fish or dried fish, herbs.
Time: 6 to 8 hours plus 30 minutes.
Method: Boil seasonal vegetables with onion, ginger, garlic, green chili, and salt. Add fermented fish or dried fish for depth. Simmer until light and brothy. Garnish with herbs and serve with rice.

Singju

Ingredients: Cabbage, lotus stem, banana flower or vegetables, roasted gram flour, sesame powder, chili, salt, herbs, fermented fish.
Time: 6 to 8 hours plus 30 minutes.
Method: Shred cabbage, lotus stem, banana flower, or seasonal vegetables. Mix with roasted gram flour, sesame powder, chili, salt, herbs, and fermented fish if desired. Toss well and serve as a spicy salad.

Nga Thongba

Ingredients: Fish, turmeric, salt, onion, ginger, garlic, tomato, chili, spices, water.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Marinate fish with turmeric and salt. Cook onion, ginger, garlic, tomato, chili, and spices in little oil. Add water and fish pieces, then simmer until the curry is light and tender.

Morok Metpa

Ingredients: Green or dry chilies, fermented fish, salt, onion, coriander, mustard oil.
Time: 6 to 8 hours plus 30 minutes.
Method: Roast green or dry chilies and mash with fermented fish, salt, onion, and coriander. Add a little mustard oil if desired. Serve as a hot chutney with rice.

Kangshoi

Ingredients: Vegetables, ginger, garlic, chili, salt, fish or fermented fish, water.
Time: 6 to 8 hours plus 30 minutes.
Method: Boil vegetables with ginger, garlic, chili, and salt. Add fish or fermented fish for flavour. Cook until vegetables are tender and broth is clear. Serve hot with rice.
