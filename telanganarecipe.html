Hyderabadi Biryani

Ingredients: Chicken or mutton, curd, ginger-garlic, chili, turmeric, biryani masala, mint, coriander, fried onion, lemon, salt, basmati rice, saffron milk, ghee.
Time: 1 hour 30 minutes.
Method: Marinate chicken or mutton with curd, ginger-garlic, chili, turmeric, biryani masala, mint, coriander, fried onion, lemon, and salt. Layer with half-cooked basmati rice, saffron milk, ghee, and fried onions. Dum cook until meat is tender.

Sarva Pindi

Ingredients: Rice flour, chana dal, sesame, peanuts, onion, green chili, ginger, curry leaves, cumin, salt, water, oil.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Mix rice flour with chana dal, sesame, peanuts, onion, green chili, ginger, curry leaves, cumin, salt, and water. Pat into a greased pan, make small holes, drizzle oil, and cook slowly until crisp.

Sakinalu

Ingredients: Rice flour, sesame, ajwain, salt, water, oil.
Time: 2 hours.
Method: Soak rice, dry it, and grind into flour. Mix with sesame, ajwain, salt, and water. Shape into spiral rings and deep-fry until crisp. Cool completely before storing.

Pachi Pulusu

Ingredients: Tamarind, onion, green chili, jaggery, salt, coriander, mustard seeds, cumin, curry leaves, dry chili.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Soak tamarind and extract juice. Mix with onion, green chili, jaggery, salt, and coriander. Temper mustard seeds, cumin, curry leaves, and dry chili, then add to the raw tamarind broth. Serve with rice.

Kodi Kura

Ingredients: Chicken, onion, ginger-garlic, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, tomato, coriander.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Cook chicken with onion, ginger-garlic, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and tomato. Add water and simmer until tender. Finish with coriander and serve with rice or roti.

Qubani Ka Meetha

Ingredients: Dried apricots, sugar, water, apricot kernels, cream or custard.
Time: Overnight soaking plus 30 minutes.
Method: Soak dried apricots overnight. Cook with sugar and water until soft and pulpy. Remove kernels, add almond-like seeds if desired, and simmer. Serve with cream or custard.
