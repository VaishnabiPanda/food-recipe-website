Poha Jalebi

Ingredients: Flattened rice, oil, mustard seeds, cumin, curry leaves, green chili, onion, turmeric, peanuts, salt, sugar, lemon juice, sev, coriander, jalebi.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Wash flattened rice and drain. Temper oil with mustard seeds, cumin, curry leaves, green chili, onion, turmeric, and peanuts. Add poha, salt, sugar, and lemon juice. Garnish with sev and coriander. Serve with hot jalebi.

Dal Bafla

Ingredients: Wheat flour, semolina, ghee, salt, spices, toor dal, tomato, cumin, garlic, chili.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Make wheat dough with semolina, ghee, salt, and spices. Shape balls, boil until cooked, then bake or roast until golden. Dip in ghee and serve with spiced dal made from toor dal, tomato, cumin, garlic, and chili.

Bhutte Ka Kees

Ingredients: Fresh corn, ghee, mustard seeds, cumin, green chili, ginger, turmeric, milk, salt, lemon juice, coriander, coconut.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Grate fresh corn. Cook in ghee with mustard seeds, cumin, green chili, ginger, turmeric, and milk. Stir until thick and soft. Add salt, lemon juice, coriander, and grated coconut before serving.

Bhopali Gosht Korma

Ingredients: Mutton, curd, ginger-garlic, chili, coriander, salt, onions, whole spices, nut paste.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Marinate mutton with curd, ginger-garlic, chili, coriander, and salt. Cook onions until golden, add mutton, whole spices, and nut paste. Simmer slowly until tender and rich. Serve with naan or rice.

Indori Garadu

Ingredients: Yam, jeeravan masala, chili powder, salt, lemon juice, oil.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Boil or fry yam cubes until crisp outside. Toss with jeeravan masala, chili powder, salt, and lemon juice. Serve hot as a winter street snack.

Mawa Bati

Ingredients: Mawa, flour, baking powder, nuts, oil, sugar syrup, cardamom.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Make dough from mawa, flour, and a little baking powder. Stuff with nuts if desired, shape balls, and deep-fry on low heat until brown. Soak in cardamom sugar syrup and garnish with nuts.
