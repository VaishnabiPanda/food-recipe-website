Dhokla

Ingredients: Gram flour, curd, water, turmeric, ginger-green chili paste, salt, fruit salt, mustard seeds, sesame, curry leaves, green chilies, sugar, lemon juice, coriander.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Make a batter with gram flour, curd, water, turmeric, ginger-green chili paste, salt, and fruit salt. Steam until fluffy. Temper mustard seeds, sesame seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, sugar, lemon juice, and water. Pour over the dhokla and garnish with coriander.

Undhiyu

Ingredients: Surti papdi, purple yam, potato, brinjal, methi muthia, coconut, coriander, sesame, peanuts, ginger, chili, spices, oil.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Prepare mixed winter vegetables like surti papdi, purple yam, potato, brinjal, and methi muthia. Stuff vegetables with coconut, coriander, sesame, peanuts, ginger, chili, and spices. Slow-cook with oil and a little water until tender. Serve with puri or rotli.

Khandvi

Ingredients: Gram flour, curd, water, turmeric, ginger-chili paste, salt, mustard seeds, sesame, curry leaves, green chili.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Whisk gram flour with curd, water, turmeric, ginger-chili paste, and salt. Cook while stirring until thick and glossy. Spread thinly on greased plates, cool, cut strips, and roll. Temper mustard seeds, sesame, curry leaves, and green chili, then pour over the rolls.

Handvo

Ingredients: Rice, lentils, bottle gourd, curd, ginger-chili paste, turmeric, chili powder, sesame, fruit salt.
Time: 6 hours soaking plus 45 minutes.
Method: Soak rice and lentils, grind into batter, and ferment. Mix with grated bottle gourd, curd, ginger-chili paste, turmeric, chili powder, sesame, and fruit salt. Bake or cook in a pan until crisp outside and soft inside. Serve with chutney.

Sev Tameta Nu Shaak

Ingredients: Tomatoes, oil, mustard seeds, cumin, ginger, green chili, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, jaggery, salt, sev.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Cook tomatoes with oil, mustard seeds, cumin, ginger, green chili, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, jaggery, and salt. Simmer until saucy and tangy-sweet. Add sev just before serving so it stays lightly crisp.

Thepla

Ingredients: Wheat flour, methi leaves, curd, turmeric, chili powder, sesame, ajwain, salt, oil, pickle or curd.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Mix wheat flour with methi leaves, curd, turmeric, chili powder, sesame, ajwain, salt, and oil. Knead into dough, roll thin, and cook on a tawa with ghee or oil. Serve with pickle or curd.
