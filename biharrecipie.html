Litti Chokha

Ingredients: Wheat flour, salt, ajwain, ghee, sattu, onion, garlic, ginger, green chili, coriander, mustard oil, lemon, pickle masala, brinjal, tomato, potato.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Make wheat dough with salt, ajwain, and ghee. Prepare stuffing with sattu, onion, garlic, ginger, green chili, coriander, mustard oil, lemon, and pickle masala. Stuff dough balls and roast or bake until browned. Serve with mashed roasted brinjal, tomato, and potato chokha.

Dal Pitha

Ingredients: Chana dal, cumin, ginger, chili, salt, rice flour, water, chutney.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Soak chana dal, grind coarsely, and saute with cumin, ginger, chili, and salt. Make rice flour dough with boiling water. Fill small dough cups with dal, seal, and steam for 10 to 15 minutes. Serve with chutney.

Sattu Paratha

Ingredients: Sattu, onion, garlic, green chili, pickle masala, mustard oil, lemon, ajwain, salt, wheat dough, ghee.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Mix sattu with onion, garlic, green chili, pickle masala, mustard oil, lemon, ajwain, and salt. Stuff into wheat dough balls, roll into parathas, and cook with ghee until crisp. Serve with curd or pickle.

Kadhi Badi

Ingredients: Gram flour, curd, turmeric, salt, water, mustard seeds, dry chili, fenugreek, asafoetida, oil.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Make small fritters from gram flour batter and fry until golden. Whisk curd with gram flour, turmeric, salt, and water, then simmer until smooth. Add the badis and cook until soft. Temper with mustard seeds, dry chili, fenugreek, and asafoetida.

Aloo Kachalu Chaat

Ingredients: Potatoes, mustard oil, black salt, roasted cumin powder, chili powder, green chili, lemon juice, tamarind, onion, coriander.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Boil potatoes and cut into cubes. Toss with mustard oil, black salt, roasted cumin powder, chili powder, green chili, lemon juice, tamarind, onion, and coriander. Serve immediately as a tangy snack.

Ghugni

Ingredients: Dried yellow peas, onion, garlic, ginger, tomato, bay leaf, cumin, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, mustard oil, coriander, lemon.
Time: Overnight soaking plus 30 minutes.
Method: Soak dried yellow peas overnight and boil until soft. Cook onion, garlic, ginger, tomato, bay leaf, cumin, turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala in mustard oil. Add peas and simmer until thick. Garnish with coriander and lemon.
