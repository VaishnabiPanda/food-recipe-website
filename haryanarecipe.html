Bajra Khichdi

Ingredients: Bajra, moong dal, turmeric, salt, water, ghee, cumin, asafoetida, ginger, green chili, curd.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Soak bajra and moong dal for a few hours. Pressure-cook with turmeric, salt, and enough water until soft. Temper ghee with cumin, asafoetida, ginger, and green chili. Mix into the khichdi and serve with curd and ghee.

Besan Masala Roti

Ingredients: Wheat flour, besan, onion, green chili, coriander, cumin, chili powder, ajwain, salt, oil, ghee.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Mix wheat flour, besan, onion, green chili, coriander, cumin, chili powder, ajwain, salt, and oil. Knead with water, roll into rotis, and cook on a tawa with ghee. Serve with curd or pickle.

Kachri Ki Sabzi

Ingredients: Dried kachri, mustard oil, cumin, asafoetida, turmeric, coriander powder, chili powder, salt, water.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Crush dried kachri and soak briefly. Cook in mustard oil with cumin, asafoetida, turmeric, coriander powder, chili powder, and salt. Add water and simmer until tangy and soft. Serve with bajra roti.

Hara Dhania Cholia

Ingredients: Green chickpeas, cumin, ginger, green chili, onion, ghee, turmeric, coriander powder, salt, coriander.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Shell fresh green chickpeas. Saute cumin, ginger, green chili, and onion in ghee. Add cholia, turmeric, coriander powder, salt, and a little water. Cook until tender and finish with fresh coriander.

Singri Ki Sabzi

Ingredients: Dried singri, mustard oil, cumin, asafoetida, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, amchur, salt.
Time: Overnight soaking plus 30 minutes.
Method: Soak dried singri overnight and boil until soft. Cook with mustard oil, cumin, asafoetida, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, amchur, and salt. Simmer until coated in spices. Serve with roti.

Meethe Chawal

Ingredients: Basmati rice, sugar, saffron, cardamom, cloves, ghee, nuts, raisins.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Cook basmati rice until almost done. Prepare sugar syrup with saffron, cardamom, cloves, and ghee. Mix rice into syrup with nuts and raisins, cover, and cook on low heat until fragrant.
