Sarson Da Saag

Ingredients: Mustard greens, spinach, bathua, green chili, ginger, garlic, salt, maize flour, ghee, onion, butter.
Time: 1 hour 15 minutes.
Method: Pressure-cook mustard greens, spinach, and bathua with green chili, ginger, garlic, and salt. Mash and simmer with maize flour to thicken. Temper ghee with onion, garlic, and chili, then mix into the greens. Serve with makki di roti and butter.

Chole

Ingredients: Chickpeas, tea bag, bay leaf, salt, onion, ginger, garlic, tomato, chole masala, cumin, coriander, chili powder, amchur.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Soak chickpeas overnight and boil with tea bag, bay leaf, and salt. Cook onion, ginger, garlic, tomato, chole masala, cumin, coriander, chili powder, and amchur until the oil separates. Add chickpeas and simmer until thick. Serve with bhature or kulcha.

Rajma

Ingredients: Rajma, onion, ginger-garlic paste, tomato, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, rice.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Soak rajma overnight and pressure-cook until soft. Prepare masala with onion, ginger-garlic paste, tomato, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala. Add rajma and simmer until creamy. Serve with steamed rice.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients: Paneer, capsicum, onion, hung curd, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, kasuri methi, lemon, salt.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Marinate paneer cubes, capsicum, and onion in hung curd, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, kasuri methi, lemon, and salt. Skewer and grill, bake, or pan-roast until charred. Serve with mint chutney.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, curd, ginger-garlic, chili powder, garam masala, lemon, salt, butter, tomato puree, cashew paste, cream, kasuri methi.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Marinate chicken with curd, ginger-garlic, chili powder, garam masala, lemon, and salt. Grill or pan-cook. Prepare a sauce with butter, tomato puree, cashew paste, cream, kasuri methi, and spices. Simmer chicken in the sauce until tender.

Makki Di Roti

Ingredients: Maize flour, warm water, salt, ghee, saag.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Knead maize flour with warm water and salt into a soft dough. Pat into flat rotis using hands or parchment. Cook on a hot tawa with ghee until brown spots appear. Serve hot with saag.
