Momos

Ingredients: Flour, water, salt, minced meat or vegetables, onion, ginger, garlic, chili, coriander, tomato chutney.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Prepare flour dough and rest it. Mix minced meat or vegetables with onion, ginger, garlic, coriander, pepper, and salt. Fill dough circles, pleat, and steam until cooked. Serve with spicy tomato chutney.

Thukpa

Ingredients: Noodles, garlic, ginger, onion, green chili, carrots, cabbage, greens, stock, salt, pepper, spring onion, coriander.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Cook noodles separately. Simmer vegetables, garlic, ginger, onion, and stock with salt and pepper. Add noodles and meat if desired. Garnish with spring onion and coriander.

Phagshapa

Ingredients: Pork strips, radish, dry red chilies, ginger, salt, rice.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Boil pork strips until tender. Cook with radish, dry red chilies, ginger, and salt. Simmer until the broth becomes mildly spicy and the radish softens. Serve with rice.

Gundruk Soup

Ingredients: Fermented leafy greens, tomato, onion, garlic, chili, salt, water.
Time: 6 to 8 hours plus 30 minutes.
Method: Soak fermented leafy greens in warm water. Boil with tomato, onion, garlic, chili, and salt. Simmer until tangy and earthy. Serve as a soup with rice.

Sha Phaley

Ingredients: Flour, water, salt, minced meat, cabbage, onion, ginger, garlic, spices, oil.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Make dough with flour, water, and salt. Fill with minced meat, cabbage, onion, ginger, garlic, and spices. Fold into half-moons and deep-fry until crisp. Serve with chutney.

Sel Roti

Ingredients: Rice, sugar, cardamom, water, oil.
Time: 4 hours soaking plus 30 minutes.
Method: Soak rice and grind with sugar, cardamom, and a little water into thick batter. Pour rings into hot oil and fry until golden. Serve during festivals or with tea.
