Bisi Bele Bath

Ingredients: Rice, toor dal, vegetables, coriander, chana dal, urad dal, dry red chili, cinnamon, cloves, coconut, tamarind, jaggery, ghee.
Time: 50 minutes.
Method: Cook rice and toor dal with vegetables. Roast and grind coriander, chana dal, urad dal, dry red chili, cinnamon, cloves, coconut, and spices. Mix the masala with tamarind, jaggery, and cooked rice-dal. Simmer until thick and finish with ghee tempering.

Mysore Masala Dosa

Ingredients: Dosa batter, potatoes, onion, mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric, red chutney, sambar, coconut chutney.
Time: Overnight fermentation plus 35 minutes.
Method: Prepare fermented dosa batter. Make potato masala with onion, mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric, and boiled potatoes. Spread dosa thin, apply red chutney, add potato filling, fold, and serve with sambar and coconut chutney.

Ragi Mudde

Ingredients: Ragi flour, water, salt, soppu saaru or curry.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Boil water with salt. Add ragi flour gradually and cook without stirring first, then mix vigorously into a smooth ball. Shape into mudde and serve hot with soppu saaru or meat curry.

Neer Dosa

Ingredients: Rice, coconut, water, salt, chutney or curry.
Time: 4 hours soaking plus 20 minutes.
Method: Soak rice, grind with coconut and water into a thin batter, and add salt. Pour on a hot pan in a thin layer without spreading. Cover and cook briefly. Serve with chutney or curry.

Mangalorean Fish Curry

Ingredients: Fish, coconut, red chilies, coriander, cumin, turmeric, garlic, tamarind, onion, water.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Grind coconut, red chilies, coriander, cumin, turmeric, garlic, and tamarind. Simmer paste with onion and water. Add fish pieces and cook gently until done. Serve with rice.

Mysore Pak

Ingredients: Ghee, sugar, gram flour.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Heat ghee and keep warm. Prepare sugar syrup to one-string consistency, add roasted gram flour gradually, and pour in ghee while stirring. Cook until porous and aromatic. Set in a greased tray and cut.
