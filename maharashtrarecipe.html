Vada Pav

Ingredients: Potatoes, garlic-chili paste, turmeric, mustard seeds, curry leaves, gram flour, pav, chutneys, oil.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Make potato filling with boiled potatoes, garlic-chili paste, turmeric, mustard seeds, curry leaves, and salt. Shape into balls, dip in gram flour batter, and deep-fry. Place in pav with dry garlic chutney and fried chili.

Misal Pav

Ingredients: Sprouted moth beans, onion, tomato, ginger-garlic, goda masala, chili powder, coconut, oil, farsan, coriander, lemon, pav.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Cook sprouted moth beans with onion, tomato, ginger-garlic, goda masala, chili powder, and coconut. Prepare spicy tari with oil and red chili. Serve sprouts with tari, farsan, onion, coriander, lemon, and pav.

Puran Poli

Ingredients: Chana dal, jaggery, cardamom, nutmeg, wheat flour, ghee.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Cook chana dal until soft, drain, and cook with jaggery, cardamom, and nutmeg until thick. Grind smooth. Stuff into wheat dough, roll gently, and cook on tawa with ghee. Serve warm.

Pav Bhaji

Ingredients: Potatoes, cauliflower, peas, capsicum, butter, onion, tomato, ginger-garlic, pav bhaji masala, chili powder, salt, pav.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Boil potatoes, cauliflower, peas, and capsicum. Mash and cook with butter, onion, tomato, ginger-garlic, pav bhaji masala, chili powder, and salt. Serve with butter-toasted pav, onion, and lemon.

Kothimbir Vadi

Ingredients: Coriander, gram flour, rice flour, sesame, chili, turmeric, cumin, salt, water, oil.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Mix chopped coriander with gram flour, rice flour, sesame, chili, turmeric, cumin, salt, and water. Steam the batter until set, slice, and shallow-fry until crisp. Serve with chutney.

Sol Kadhi

Ingredients: Coconut milk, kokum water, garlic, green chili, cumin, salt, coriander.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Extract coconut milk and mix with kokum water, garlic, green chili, cumin, and salt. Chill and garnish with coriander. Serve as a cooling drink with rice or fish meals.
