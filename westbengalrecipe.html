Shorshe Ilish

Ingredients: Hilsa, turmeric, salt, mustard seeds, green chili, mustard oil, water.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Marinate hilsa with turmeric and salt. Grind mustard seeds with green chili and a little salt. Place fish with mustard paste, mustard oil, turmeric, chili, and water. Steam or simmer gently until cooked. Serve with rice.

Kosha Mangsho

Ingredients: Mutton, curd, ginger-garlic, turmeric, chili, salt, onions, spices, water.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Marinate mutton with curd, ginger-garlic, turmeric, chili, and salt. Cook onions slowly until brown, add spices and mutton, then bhuno until oil separates. Add little water and cook until tender and dark.

Luchi Alur Dom

Ingredients: Flour, salt, ghee, oil, potatoes, tomato, ginger, cumin, coriander, chili, turmeric, garam masala.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Make soft dough with flour, salt, and ghee. Roll small discs and deep-fry until puffed. For alur dom, cook potatoes with tomato, ginger, cumin, coriander, chili, turmeric, and garam masala. Serve together.

Chingri Malai Curry

Ingredients: Prawns, turmeric, salt, onion paste, ginger, garlic, whole spices, coconut milk, ghee, garam masala.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Marinate prawns with turmeric and salt, then lightly fry. Cook onion paste, ginger, garlic, whole spices, and coconut milk. Add prawns and simmer briefly. Finish with ghee and garam masala.

Shukto

Ingredients: Bitter gourd, mixed vegetables, ginger, mustard-poppy paste, milk, mild spices, panch phoron or radhuni.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Cook bitter gourd and mixed vegetables with ginger, mustard-poppy paste, milk, and mild spices. Temper with panch phoron or radhuni. Simmer gently for a slightly bitter, creamy starter.

Rasgulla

Ingredients: Milk, lemon juice, sugar, water.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Curdle milk with lemon juice, strain chhena, and knead until smooth. Shape balls and cook in light sugar syrup until spongy. Cool in syrup before serving.
