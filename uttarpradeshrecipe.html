Awadhi Biryani

Ingredients: Meat, curd, ginger-garlic, spices, mint, coriander, fried onion, rice, saffron milk, ghee, kewra.
Time: 1 hour 30 minutes.
Method: Marinate meat with curd, ginger-garlic, spices, mint, coriander, and fried onion. Cook until partly done. Layer with parboiled rice, saffron milk, ghee, and kewra if desired. Dum cook on low heat until fragrant.

Tunday Kebab

Ingredients: Minced meat, papaya paste, fried onion paste, ginger-garlic, roasted gram flour, ghee, kebab spices, paratha.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Mix finely minced meat with papaya paste, fried onion paste, ginger-garlic, roasted gram flour, ghee, and kebab spices. Rest the mixture, shape into patties, and shallow-fry until soft and browned. Serve with paratha.

Bedmi Puri

Ingredients: Urad dal, fennel, cumin, chili, asafoetida, salt, wheat flour, oil, aloo sabzi.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Grind soaked urad dal with fennel, cumin, chili, asafoetida, and salt. Mix into wheat flour dough. Roll small puris and deep-fry until puffed. Serve with aloo sabzi.

Aloo Rasedar

Ingredients: Potatoes, tomato, ginger, green chili, cumin, turmeric, coriander, chili powder, salt, water.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Boil potatoes and break into chunks. Cook tomato, ginger, green chili, cumin, turmeric, coriander, chili powder, and salt. Add potatoes and water, then simmer into a thin spicy curry. Serve with puri.

Basket Chaat

Ingredients: Potato baskets or katori shells, potato, chickpeas, curd, tamarind chutney, green chutney, chaat masala, sev, onion, coriander.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Make crispy potato baskets or use fried katori shells. Fill with boiled potato, chickpeas, curd, tamarind chutney, green chutney, chaat masala, sev, onion, and coriander. Serve immediately.

Petha

Ingredients: Ash gourd, lime water, sugar syrup, cardamom or rose essence.
Time: 2 hours.
Method: Peel ash gourd and cut into cubes. Soak in lime water, rinse well, and boil until translucent. Cook in sugar syrup with cardamom or rose essence until glossy. Dry slightly before serving.
