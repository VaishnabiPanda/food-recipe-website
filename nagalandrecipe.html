Smoked Pork with Bamboo Shoot

Ingredients: Smoked pork, bamboo shoot, ginger, garlic, salt, Naga chili, water.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Boil smoked pork with bamboo shoot, ginger, garlic, and salt until tender. Add Naga chili carefully for heat. Simmer until the fat flavours the broth. Serve with rice.

Axone Pork

Ingredients: Pork, fermented soybean paste, ginger, garlic, chili, salt, water.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Cook pork with fermented soybean paste, ginger, garlic, chili, and salt. Add water and simmer until the pork is tender and the sauce is thick and aromatic. Serve hot with rice.

Galho

Ingredients: Rice, vegetables, smoked meat or pork, ginger, garlic, green chili, salt, herbs.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Cook rice with vegetables, smoked meat or pork, ginger, garlic, green chili, and salt in plenty of water. Simmer until it becomes a soft porridge. Garnish with herbs.

Anishi Curry

Ingredients: Dried yam leaves, pork, ginger, garlic, chili, salt.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Use dried yam leaves and soak briefly. Cook with pork, ginger, garlic, chili, and salt. Simmer until leaves soften and curry becomes earthy. Serve with rice.

Fish in Bamboo Shoot

Ingredients: Fish, bamboo shoot, ginger, garlic, chili, salt, water.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Boil fish with bamboo shoot, ginger, garlic, chili, and salt. Keep spices minimal so the bamboo shoot flavour stays clear. Simmer gently until fish is cooked.

Hinkejvu

Ingredients: Cabbage, colocasia leaves, beans, vegetables, salt, pork fat or smoked meat.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Boil cabbage, colocasia leaves, beans, and other vegetables with a little salt. Add pork fat or smoked meat if desired. Serve as a simple vegetable stew with rice.
