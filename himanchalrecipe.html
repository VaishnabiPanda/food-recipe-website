Madra

Ingredients: Chickpeas or rajma, curd, gram flour, ghee, cumin, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, turmeric, coriander, asafoetida, salt.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Soak chickpeas or rajma and boil until tender. Whisk curd with gram flour and spices. Cook ghee with cumin, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, turmeric, coriander, and asafoetida. Add curd slowly, stir continuously, add beans, and simmer until thick.

Siddu

Ingredients: Wheat flour, yeast, urad dal or walnuts, spices, ghee, dal or chutney.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Make a soft yeast dough with wheat flour and let it rise. Prepare a filling of ground urad dal or walnuts with spices. Stuff dough balls, shape them, and steam until fluffy. Serve hot with ghee, dal, or chutney.

Dham

Ingredients: Rice, madra, rajma, dal, boor ki kari, sweet rice, ghee, curd, mild spices.
Time: 2 hours.
Method: Prepare rice, madra, rajma, dal, boor ki kari, and sweet rice separately with ghee, curd, and mild spices. Serve in a traditional sequence on leaf plates. The key is slow cooking and balanced use of yogurt-based gravies.

Tudkiya Bhath

Ingredients: Rice, lentils, potato, curd, onion, tomato, ginger-garlic, bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom, turmeric, chili, lemon, coriander.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Cook rice with soaked lentils, potato, curd, onion, tomato, ginger-garlic, bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom, turmeric, and chili. Simmer or pressure-cook until fragrant and soft. Finish with lemon juice and coriander.

Babru

Ingredients: Wheat flour, black gram, fennel, salt, spices, oil, tamarind chutney or potato curry.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Make wheat dough and fill it with a paste of soaked black gram, fennel, salt, and spices. Roll carefully and deep-fry or shallow-fry until crisp and golden. Serve with tamarind chutney or potato curry.

Chana Madra

Ingredients: Chickpeas, ghee, whole spices, curd, turmeric, coriander, garam masala, salt.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Boil chickpeas and cook them in ghee with whole spices. Add beaten curd gradually while stirring so it does not split. Simmer with turmeric, coriander, garam masala, and salt until creamy. Serve with rice.
