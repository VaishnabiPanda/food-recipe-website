Khar

Ingredients: Khar water, raw papaya, pulses, mustard oil, bay leaf, salt.
Time: 45 minutes.
Method: Prepare khar water from sun-dried banana peel ash, or use ready khar. Boil raw papaya and pulses until soft. Cook in mustard oil with bay leaf, salt, and khar water. Simmer gently until the vegetables absorb the flavour. Serve at the start of a meal.

Aloo Pitika

Ingredients: Potatoes, mustard oil, onion, green chili, coriander, salt, roasted garlic or chili.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Boil potatoes and mash them while warm. Mix with mustard oil, chopped onion, green chili, coriander, and salt. Add roasted garlic or chili for smoky depth. Serve with rice and dal.

Ou Tenga Dal

Ingredients: Toor dal, elephant apple, turmeric, salt, mustard oil, mustard seeds, dry red chili, garlic.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Boil toor dal until soft. Cook elephant apple slices with turmeric and salt. Combine dal and fruit, then simmer. Temper mustard oil with mustard seeds, dry red chili, and garlic. Pour over the dal and serve with rice.

Bilahir Tenga

Ingredients: Tomatoes, mustard oil, mustard seeds, green chili, turmeric, salt, water, lentil dumplings.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Saute tomatoes in mustard oil with mustard seeds, green chili, turmeric, and salt. Add water and simmer until tangy and light. Add fried lentil dumplings if desired. Serve with plain rice.

Lau Tenga

Ingredients: Bottle gourd, mustard seeds, green chili, turmeric, salt, lemon juice or tomato, water.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Peel and cube bottle gourd. Saute with mustard seeds, green chili, turmeric, and salt. Add water and a souring ingredient like lemon juice or tomato. Simmer until the gourd is tender and the curry is light.

Kosu Xaak with Black Gram

Ingredients: Colocasia leaves, urad dal, turmeric, salt, mustard oil, mustard seeds, garlic, dry red chili.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Wash and chop colocasia leaves. Boil with soaked urad dal, turmeric, and salt until soft. Temper mustard oil with mustard seeds, garlic, and dry red chili, then mix into the greens. Serve with rice.
