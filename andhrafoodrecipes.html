Pulihora

Ingredients: Rice, tamarind, turmeric, salt, jaggery, green chilies, mustard seeds, chana dal, urad dal, dry red chilies, curry leaves, peanuts, oil.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Cook rice and cool it on a plate. Soak tamarind, extract thick pulp, and cook it with turmeric, salt, jaggery, and green chilies. Temper oil with mustard seeds, chana dal, urad dal, dry red chilies, curry leaves, and peanuts. Mix the tamarind paste into rice and rest for 30 minutes before serving.

Dondakaya Fry

Ingredients: Ivy gourd, oil, mustard seeds, cumin, urad dal, curry leaves, turmeric, chili powder, salt, garam masala, peanut powder or coconut.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Slice ivy gourd thinly. Heat oil and temper mustard seeds, cumin, urad dal, and curry leaves. Add ivy gourd, turmeric, chili powder, salt, and a little garam masala. Fry until cooked and lightly crisp. Finish with roasted peanut powder or coconut if desired.

Gongura Pickle

Ingredients: Gongura leaves, fenugreek, coriander seeds, dry red chilies, salt, oil, mustard seeds, garlic, curry leaves.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Wash gongura leaves and saute them until wilted. Roast fenugreek, coriander seeds, and dry red chilies, then grind with the leaves and salt. Heat oil and temper mustard seeds, garlic, and curry leaves. Mix the tempering into the chutney and serve with hot rice.

Pesarattu

Ingredients: Whole green gram, rice, ginger, cumin, green chilies, salt, oil, ginger chutney or upma.
Time: 6 hours soaking plus 25 minutes.
Method: Soak whole green gram with a little rice for several hours. Grind with ginger, cumin, green chilies, and salt into a smooth batter. Spread on a hot tawa like dosa, drizzle oil, and cook until crisp. Serve with ginger chutney or upma.

Medu Vada

Ingredients: Urad dal, onion, green chili, ginger, pepper, curry leaves, salt, oil, sambar, coconut chutney.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Soak urad dal, drain, and grind into a thick fluffy batter. Mix with onion, green chili, ginger, pepper, curry leaves, and salt. Shape into rings and deep-fry until golden outside and soft inside. Serve with sambar and coconut chutney.

Punugulu

Ingredients: Dosa or idli batter, onion, green chili, ginger, curry leaves, salt, rice flour, oil, peanut chutney.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Use slightly sour dosa or idli batter. Mix in chopped onion, green chili, ginger, curry leaves, salt, and a little rice flour for crispness. Drop spoonfuls into hot oil and fry until golden. Serve with peanut chutney.

Curd Rice

Ingredients: Rice, curd, salt, milk, mustard seeds, urad dal, green chili, ginger, curry leaves, coriander or pomegranate.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Cook rice until soft and mash lightly after cooling. Mix with curd, salt, and a splash of milk. Temper mustard seeds, urad dal, green chili, ginger, and curry leaves, then pour over the rice. Garnish with coriander or pomegranate.
