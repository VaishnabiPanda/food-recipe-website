Chila

Ingredients: Rice flour, water, salt, onion, green chili, coriander, oil, chutney.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Make a thin batter with rice flour, water, salt, chopped onion, green chili, and coriander. Rest for 10 minutes. Pour on a hot tawa like dosa, drizzle oil, and cook both sides until lightly crisp. Serve with tomato chutney or green chutney.

Fara

Ingredients: Rice, water, mustard seeds, curry leaves, sesame, green chili, oil.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Soak rice, grind into paste, and cook with a little water until it becomes dough-like. Shape into small dumplings or cylinders. Steam until firm. Temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, sesame, and green chili in oil, toss the fara, and serve hot.

Muthia

Ingredients: Rice flour, greens, garlic, chili, cumin, salt, curd, mustard seeds, sesame.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Mix rice flour with chopped greens, garlic, chili, cumin, salt, and a little curd or water. Shape into small rolls and steam until cooked. Slice and temper with mustard seeds and sesame. Serve as a snack or side dish.

Bafauri

Ingredients: Chana dal, ginger, garlic, green chili, cumin, salt, onion, coriander, chutney.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Soak chana dal, grind coarsely with ginger, garlic, green chili, cumin, and salt. Mix with chopped onion and coriander. Shape small portions and steam until firm. Serve with chutney as a healthy steamed snack.

Aamat

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, ginger, garlic, green chili, turmeric, salt, bamboo shoot, water, coriander.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Cook mixed vegetables with ginger, garlic, green chili, turmeric, and salt. Add soaked and cooked bamboo shoot if available. Simmer with water until the vegetables soften. Finish with coriander and serve with rice.

Dubki Kadhi

Ingredients: Curd, gram flour, turmeric, salt, water, mustard seeds, cumin, curry leaves, dry chili.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Whisk curd, gram flour, turmeric, salt, and water. Make small gram flour dumplings and drop them into the simmering kadhi. Cook until dumplings are soft. Temper with mustard seeds, cumin, curry leaves, and dry chili.
