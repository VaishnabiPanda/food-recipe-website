Dahi Baingan

Ingredients: Brinjal, turmeric, chili powder, salt, curd, roasted cumin, sugar, mustard seeds, curry leaves, dry red chili, asafoetida.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Slice brinjal, season it with turmeric, chili powder, and salt, then shallow-fry until soft and golden. Whisk curd with roasted cumin, salt, and a little sugar. Arrange the brinjal over the curd and finish with a tempering of mustard seeds, curry leaves, dry red chili, and asafoetida. Serve chilled or at room temperature with rice or roti.

Khechudi

Ingredients: Rice, moong dal, turmeric, salt, ginger, bay leaf, cumin, potato, carrot, peas, ghee, cashews, roasted cumin powder.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Wash rice and moong dal, then cook them with turmeric, salt, ginger, bay leaf, cumin, and vegetables like potato, carrot, and peas. Simmer until soft and slightly mushy. Finish with ghee, fried cashews, and roasted cumin powder. It is usually served with dalma, tomato khatta, or curd.

Dalma

Ingredients: Toor dal, pumpkin, raw banana, papaya, brinjal, turmeric, salt, ginger, ghee, panch phoron, dry red chili, bay leaf.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Pressure-cook toor dal with vegetables such as pumpkin, raw banana, papaya, and brinjal. Add turmeric, salt, and ginger while cooking. Temper ghee with panch phoron, dry red chili, and bay leaf, then pour it over the cooked dal. Sprinkle roasted cumin and chili powder before serving with rice.

Pakhala Bhata

Ingredients: Rice, water, curd, salt, mustard seeds, curry leaves, garlic, dry red chili, mustard oil.
Time: Overnight soaking plus 30 minutes.
Method: Cook rice and let it cool. Add water and allow it to rest for a few hours, or overnight for a light fermented taste. Add curd if desired and season with salt. Temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, garlic, and dry red chili in mustard oil and pour over the rice. Serve with fried vegetables, badi chura, or fish.

Chhena Poda

Ingredients: Chhena, sugar, semolina, cardamom, cashews, raisins, ghee, banana leaf.
Time: 1 hour 15 minutes.
Method: Knead fresh chhena until smooth, then mix with sugar, semolina, cardamom, cashews, and raisins. Pour into a greased baking tin lined with caramel or banana leaf. Bake until the outside turns deep brown and the inside sets. Cool completely before slicing.

Adisa Pitha

Ingredients: Rice flour, hot water, jaggery, coconut, cardamom, oil.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Prepare rice flour dough with hot water and make a filling of jaggery, coconut, and cardamom. Flatten small dough portions, fill them, seal carefully, and deep-fry until golden. Serve warm during festivals or with tea.
