Idli Sambar

Ingredients: Rice, urad dal, toor dal, vegetables, tamarind, sambar powder, salt, mustard seeds, curry leaves, dry chili.
Time: Overnight fermentation plus 45 minutes.
Method: Soak rice and urad dal separately, grind, mix, and ferment overnight. Steam batter in idli molds. Cook toor dal with vegetables, tamarind, sambar powder, and salt. Temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, and dry chili, then serve with idli.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients: Dosa batter, potatoes, mustard seeds, curry leaves, onion, turmeric, green chili, chutney.
Time: Overnight fermentation plus 35 minutes.
Method: Use fermented dosa batter. Prepare potato masala with mustard seeds, curry leaves, onion, turmeric, green chili, and boiled potatoes. Spread batter thin on tawa, cook crisp, place filling, fold, and serve with chutney.

Chettinad Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, fennel, pepper, coriander, cumin, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, dry chilies, coconut, onion, tomato, ginger-garlic, curry leaves.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Roast fennel, pepper, coriander, cumin, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, and dry chilies. Grind with coconut. Cook chicken with onion, tomato, ginger-garlic, curry leaves, and the masala until thick and spicy.

Pongal

Ingredients: Moong dal, rice, water, salt, ginger, ghee, pepper, cumin, curry leaves, cashews, asafoetida.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Dry-roast moong dal and cook with rice, water, salt, and ginger until soft. Temper ghee with pepper, cumin, curry leaves, cashews, and asafoetida. Mix into the pongal and serve hot.

Rasam

Ingredients: Tamarind, tomato, turmeric, rasam powder, pepper, cumin, garlic, salt, dal water, mustard seeds, curry leaves, dry chili, coriander.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Boil tamarind water with tomato, turmeric, rasam powder, pepper, cumin, garlic, and salt. Add cooked dal water if desired. Temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, and dry chili. Garnish with coriander.

Payasam

Ingredients: Vermicelli or rice, milk, sugar or jaggery, cardamom, cashews, raisins, ghee.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Cook rice, vermicelli, or dal in milk or coconut milk. Sweeten with jaggery or sugar. Add cardamom and ghee-fried cashews and raisins. Simmer until creamy.
