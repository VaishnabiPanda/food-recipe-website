Appam with Stew

Ingredients: Rice, coconut, cooked rice, yeast, sugar, salt, vegetables or chicken, coconut milk, ginger, green chili, pepper, curry leaves.
Time: Overnight fermentation plus 45 minutes.
Method: Soak rice and grind with coconut, cooked rice, yeast, sugar, and salt. Ferment until bubbly. Cook appams in a curved pan. For stew, simmer vegetables or chicken in thin coconut milk with ginger, green chili, pepper, and curry leaves, then finish with thick coconut milk.

Puttu Kadala

Ingredients: Rice flour, salted water, coconut, black chickpeas, roasted coconut, onion, tomato, spices.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Moisten rice flour with salted water until crumbly. Layer with grated coconut in a puttu maker and steam. For kadala curry, cook black chickpeas and simmer with roasted coconut, onion, tomato, and spices. Serve together.

Avial

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, turmeric, salt, coconut, cumin, green chili, curd, curry leaves, coconut oil.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Cook mixed vegetables with turmeric, salt, and little water. Grind coconut, cumin, and green chili into paste. Mix into vegetables and simmer gently. Finish with curd, curry leaves, and coconut oil.

Kerala Fish Molee

Ingredients: Fish, turmeric, salt, onion, ginger, garlic, green chili, pepper, curry leaves, coconut oil, coconut milk, tomato.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Marinate fish with turmeric and salt, then lightly fry. Cook onion, ginger, garlic, green chili, pepper, and curry leaves in coconut oil. Add thin coconut milk, fish, and tomato. Simmer gently and finish with thick coconut milk.

Malabar Biryani

Ingredients: Short-grain rice, whole spices, chicken, onion, ginger-garlic, tomato, curd, mint, coriander, ghee, fried onions, nuts, raisins.
Time: 1 hour 20 minutes.
Method: Cook short-grain rice with whole spices until partly done. Prepare chicken masala with onion, ginger-garlic, tomato, curd, mint, coriander, and spices. Layer rice and masala with ghee, fried onions, nuts, and raisins. Dum cook until fragrant.

Payasam

Ingredients: Vermicelli or rice, milk, sugar or jaggery, cardamom, cashews, raisins, ghee.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Roast vermicelli or use rice, then simmer with milk until soft. Add sugar or jaggery, cardamom, and ghee-fried cashews and raisins. Cook until creamy and serve warm or chilled.
