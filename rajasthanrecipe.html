Dal Baati Churma

Ingredients: Wheat flour, semolina, ghee, salt, mixed dal, turmeric, chili, cumin, garlic, jaggery.
Time: 1 hour 15 minutes.
Method: Make stiff wheat dough with semolina, ghee, salt, and water. Shape baatis and bake until crisp. Cook mixed dal with turmeric, chili, cumin, garlic, and ghee tempering. Crush some baati with ghee and jaggery for churma. Serve together.

Gatte Ki Sabzi

Ingredients: Gram flour, spices, curd, oil, cumin, asafoetida, turmeric, chili powder, coriander, salt.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Make gram flour dough with spices, curd, and oil. Roll into logs, boil, and slice. Cook yogurt gravy with cumin, asafoetida, turmeric, chili powder, coriander, and salt. Add gatte and simmer until coated.

Laal Maas

Ingredients: Mutton, curd, red chili, garlic, salt, onions, ghee, whole spices, mathania chili paste.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Marinate mutton with curd, red chili, garlic, and salt. Cook onions in ghee, add whole spices and mutton, then simmer with mathania chili paste until tender and deep red. Serve with bajra roti.

Ker Sangri

Ingredients: Ker, sangri, mustard oil, cumin, asafoetida, turmeric, chili powder, coriander, amchur, salt.
Time: Overnight soaking plus 30 minutes.
Method: Soak dried ker and sangri overnight, then boil. Cook in mustard oil with cumin, asafoetida, turmeric, chili powder, coriander, amchur, and salt. Simmer until tangy and dry.

Pyaaz Kachori

Ingredients: Onion, gram flour, fennel, coriander, chili, amchur, flour dough, oil, chutney.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Prepare spiced onion filling with gram flour, fennel, coriander, chili, and amchur. Stuff into flour dough, seal, and deep-fry slowly until flaky and golden. Serve with chutney.

Ghevar

Ingredients: Flour, ghee, milk, chilled water, sugar syrup, rabri, nuts.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Make thin batter with flour, ghee, milk, and chilled water. Pour into hot ghee in a mold to create a honeycomb disc. Fry crisp, soak lightly in sugar syrup, and top with rabri and nuts.
