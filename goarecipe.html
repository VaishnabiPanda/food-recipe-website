Goan Fish Curry

Ingredients: Fish, salt, turmeric, coconut, red chilies, coriander seeds, cumin, garlic, tamarind, onion, green chili, water.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Marinate fish with salt and turmeric. Grind coconut, red chilies, coriander seeds, cumin, garlic, turmeric, and tamarind into paste. Simmer the paste with water, onion, and green chili. Add fish and cook gently until done. Serve with steamed rice.

Pork Vindaloo

Ingredients: Pork, vinegar, garlic, ginger, red chili, cumin, cinnamon, cloves, pepper, salt, onion, water.
Time: 1 hour 15 minutes.
Method: Marinate pork with vinegar, garlic, ginger, red chili, cumin, cinnamon, cloves, pepper, and salt. Fry onions until golden, add pork, and cook until browned. Add marinade and water, then simmer until tender and thick. Rest before serving for deeper flavour.

Xacuti

Ingredients: Chicken, coconut, poppy seeds, coriander, cumin, cloves, cinnamon, pepper, red chilies, onion, ginger, garlic, turmeric.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Roast coconut, poppy seeds, coriander, cumin, cloves, cinnamon, pepper, and red chilies. Grind to paste. Cook chicken with onion, ginger, garlic, turmeric, and the spice paste. Simmer until the gravy is rich. Serve with pao or rice.

Bebinca

Ingredients: Coconut milk, egg yolks, sugar, flour, nutmeg, ghee.
Time: 2 hours.
Method: Prepare batter with coconut milk, egg yolks, sugar, flour, nutmeg, and ghee. Pour a thin layer into a greased tin and bake until set. Brush ghee, add another layer, and repeat until many layers form. Cool and slice.

Prawn Balchao

Ingredients: Prawns, salt, turmeric, red chilies, vinegar, cumin, mustard, ginger, garlic, onion, tomato.
Time: 35 minutes.
Method: Clean prawns and marinate with salt and turmeric. Grind red chilies, vinegar, cumin, mustard, ginger, and garlic. Fry onion and tomato, add masala, then prawns. Cook until thick and pickle-like. Serve with rice.

Sorpotel

Ingredients: Pork, liver, vinegar, red chilies, garlic, cumin, cinnamon, cloves, pepper, salt.
Time: 1 hour 30 minutes.
Method: Boil pork and liver until partly cooked, then dice. Fry pieces lightly. Prepare a masala with vinegar, red chilies, garlic, cumin, cinnamon, cloves, and pepper. Simmer meat in the masala until tender and spicy.
