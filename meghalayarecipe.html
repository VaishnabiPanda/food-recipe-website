Jadoh

Ingredients: Rice, pork, onion, ginger, garlic, turmeric, black pepper, salt, stock, herbs.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Wash rice and cook it with pork pieces, onion, ginger, garlic, turmeric, black pepper, and salt. Add stock and simmer until rice absorbs the flavour. Garnish with herbs and serve hot.

Dohneiiong

Ingredients: Pork, black sesame, onion, ginger, garlic, chili, turmeric, salt.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Boil pork until partly cooked. Roast black sesame and grind to paste. Cook pork with onion, ginger, garlic, chili, turmeric, and sesame paste. Simmer until thick and nutty. Serve with rice.

Tungrymbai

Ingredients: Fermented soybeans, onion, ginger, garlic, chili, pork or sesame.
Time: 6 to 8 hours plus 30 minutes.
Method: Ferment soybeans until strong and soft. Cook with onion, ginger, garlic, chili, and pork or sesame if desired. Mash slightly and simmer until aromatic. Serve as a pungent side with rice.

Nakham Bitchi

Ingredients: Dry fish, water, green chili, ginger, garlic, salt, pepper.
Time: 40 minutes.
Method: Roast or dry fish, then simmer with water, green chili, ginger, garlic, and salt. Add pepper for heat. Serve as a warming soup before meals.

Pumaloi

Ingredients: Powdered rice, water, pork curry or vegetables.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Wash powdered rice and steam it in a special pot or steamer until fluffy. Do not add heavy spices. Serve with pork curry, vegetables, or chutney.

Dohkhlieh

Ingredients: Pork, onion, green chili, coriander, salt, lemon juice, tomato.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Boil pork and dice it. Mix with onion, green chili, coriander, salt, and lemon juice. Add chopped tomato if desired. Serve as a fresh pork salad.
