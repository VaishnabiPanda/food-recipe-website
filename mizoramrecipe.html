Bai

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, leafy greens, bamboo shoot, beans, salt, rice water, pork or fermented soybean.
Time: 6 to 8 hours plus 30 minutes.
Method: Boil mixed vegetables, leafy greens, bamboo shoot, and beans with salt and a little rice water. Add pork or fermented soybean if desired. Simmer until everything is tender. Serve with rice.

Sawhchiar

Ingredients: Rice, chicken or pork, ginger, garlic, onion, salt, stock, spring onion.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Cook rice with chicken or pork, ginger, garlic, onion, and salt until soft like porridge. Add stock as needed and simmer until comforting and thick. Garnish with spring onion.

Vawksa Rep

Ingredients: Smoked pork, bamboo shoot, green chili, salt, leafy greens.
Time: 1 hour.
Method: Use smoked pork cut into pieces. Boil with bamboo shoot, green chili, and salt until tender. Add leafy greens near the end. Serve smoky and simple with rice.

Chhum Han

Ingredients: Cabbage, beans, pumpkin, greens, salt, ginger or chili.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Steam or boil mixed vegetables such as cabbage, beans, pumpkin, and greens with salt. Add ginger or chili if desired. Serve as a healthy side dish with rice.

Bekang

Ingredients: Fermented soybeans, chili, ginger, onion, salt.
Time: 6 to 8 hours plus 30 minutes.
Method: Use fermented soybeans and cook with chili, ginger, onion, and a little salt. Mash lightly into a thick relish. Serve with rice and boiled vegetables.

Misa Mach Poora

Ingredients: Shrimp, salt, turmeric, chili, mustard oil, banana leaf.
Time: 30 minutes.
Method: Marinate shrimp with salt, turmeric, chili, and mustard oil. Wrap in banana leaf or grill directly until smoky and cooked. Serve with rice and chutney.
